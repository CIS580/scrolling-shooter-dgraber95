Play Scrolling Shooter

Instructions

1. Use ←, ↑, → and ↓; or 'a', 'w', 'd', and 's' to steer ship

2. Press SPACEBAR to fire

3. Collect powerups to boost firepower

4. Dodge and destroy enemies to score points

5. Destroy all enemies to progress to next level

6. Press 'p' to pause and unpause game
